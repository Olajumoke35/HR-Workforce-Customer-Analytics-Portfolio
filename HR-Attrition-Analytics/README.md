# HR, Workforce & Customer Operations Analytics Portfolio

Welcome to my Data Analytics Portfolio.

This repository contains analytics projects focused on **HR Analytics, Workforce Planning, and Customer Operations Performance**.

The goal of these projects is to demonstrate how data analysis can support **better workforce decisions, operational efficiency, and customer service performance**.

---

## Tools & Technologies

The projects in this portfolio use the following tools:

- SQL – Data extraction and analytical queries
- Excel – Data cleaning, pivot tables, and exploratory analysis
- Power BI – Interactive dashboards and visualisation
- Data storytelling – Communicating insights for decision-making

---

## Projects

### 1. HR Attrition Analytics
Analysis of employee turnover patterns to identify possible drivers of workforce attrition.

Key focus areas:
- Attrition by department
- Overtime vs attrition
- Tenure vs turnover risk

---

### 2. Workforce Capacity Planning
Workforce analytics project examining staffing levels and workload distribution.

Key insights include:
- Productivity trends across teams
- Workforce utilisation
- Potential staffing gaps

---

### 3. Customer Service Analytics
Analysis of customer support performance metrics.

Focus areas include:
- Average response time
- Ticket resolution efficiency
- Agent performance comparison

---

### 4. Recruitment Pipeline Analytics
Analysis of recruitment data to understand hiring efficiency.

Metrics analysed include:
- Time-to-hire
- Conversion rates across recruitment stages
- Recruitment source effectiveness

---

### 5. Employee Performance Analytics
Evaluation of workforce productivity and performance distribution.

Key insights include:
- Performance score distribution
- Training vs productivity patterns
- Department-level performance comparison
